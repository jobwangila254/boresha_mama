# Boresha-Mama Mother App Training Guide

## Session 1: Getting Started (20 min)
1. **Install the app** from Google Play Store
2. **Register** using your phone number
3. **Login** with phone + password
4. Choose language: **English or Kiswahili**

## Session 2: Home Screen (15 min)
- Your pregnancy week and due date
- Risk level indicator
- Quick action buttons
- Danger sign alerts (red box)
- Upcoming appointments

## Session 3: Self-Monitoring (20 min)
- Record your weight
- Check your blood pressure
- Track symptoms
- Log fetal movements
- **Why it matters:** Early detection of problems

## Session 4: Appointments (15 min)
- Book appointment at your facility
- Choose visit type
- View appointment history
- **You'll get an SMS reminder!**

## Session 5: Danger Signs (15 min)
**Contact your CHV or clinic immediately if:**
- Severe headache
- Blurred vision
- Bleeding
- Severe stomach pain
- Fits/convulsions
- Baby stops moving

**Emergency: Call 1196**

## Session 6: Profile Settings (10 min)
- Edit your name
- Switch language
- Emergency contact
- Change password

## Key Benefits
✅ Fewer trips to the clinic
✅ Track your pregnancy progress
✅ Get health tips for your stage
✅ Receive appointment reminders
✅ Early warning for danger signs
✅ Your records are safe in the cloud
