# Boresha-Mama Facility Dashboard - User Manual

**Access:** https://facility.boreshamama.go.ke

## Login
Use credentials provided by the county health department.

## Dashboard
- **Total Patients:** All registered pregnancies at your facility
- **Today's Appointments:** Appointments scheduled for today
- **Pending Referrals:** Referrals awaiting action
- **Active Pregnancies:** Currently active cases

## Patient Records
- Search patients by name or phone
- Filter by risk level
- View patient pregnancy timeline
- Access visit history and self-monitoring data

## Appointment Management
- **View** all appointments (scheduled, completed, cancelled)
- **Filter** by status
- **Complete or cancel** appointments
- System sends SMS reminders automatically

## Referral Tracking
- **Incoming:** Referrals from CHVs
- **Accept** referrals to assign to your facility
- **Complete** referrals after treatment
- Track referral outcomes

## Reports
- View clinic statistics
- Export data as CSV:
  - Pregnancy records
  - Referral records
  - CHV performance data

## Tips
- Keep patient information confidential
- Update appointment status promptly
- Document referral outcomes for tracking
- Use filters to find specific records quickly
