# Boresha-Mama Mother App - User Manual

**Language:** English / Kiswahili

## Getting Started

1. **Download** the Boresha-Mama app from Google Play Store
2. **Register** using your phone number
3. **Login** with your phone and password

## Features

### Home Screen
- View your pregnancy week and due date
- See your risk level (low/medium/high)
- Quick access to record measurements, book appointments, find facilities
- Danger sign alerts
- Upcoming appointments list

### Self-Monitoring
- Record weight, blood pressure, blood sugar
- Track symptoms and fetal movements
- View your measurement history
- Automatic danger sign alerts if readings are concerning

### Appointments
- Book appointments at your preferred facility
- Choose visit type (antenatal, postnatal, follow-up, emergency)
- View upcoming and past appointments
- Receive SMS reminders before appointments

### Health Tips
- Week-by-week pregnancy information
- Nutrition and exercise guidance
- Danger sign awareness
- Available in English and Kiswahili

### Find Facility
- View nearby health facilities on map
- See facility details (type, level, contact)
- Get directions to facilities

### Profile
- Edit personal information
- Switch language (English/Kiswahili)
- Emergency contact: Call 1196
- Change password

## Danger Signs

Contact your CHV or clinic immediately if you experience:
- Severe headache
- Blurred vision
- Vaginal bleeding
- Severe abdominal pain
- Convulsions
- High fever
- Swelling of hands/face
- Reduced fetal movements

**Emergency: Call 1196**

## Privacy

Your health data is encrypted and secure. Only you and your assigned healthcare providers can access your records. Boresha-Mama complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.
